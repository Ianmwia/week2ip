Welcome to the Akan Birthday Page

This website will help you know your Akan Name.Akan Names are a part of the culture of the Ghana people.Akan people name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These names have further meanings concerning the soul and character of a person.

Most Ghanaians have at least one name from this system in addition to their Christian or English name.Famous Notable figures include Ghana's First president Kwame Nkumar and the former UN Secretary General Kofi Annan.
